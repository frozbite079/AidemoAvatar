## 🎓 Key Learnings

1. **LiveKit is NOT a silver bullet**
   - It solves communication, not AI/documents
   - 80-85% of your work is document processing
   - LiveKit handles only 10-15%

2. **Real-time optimization beats infrastructure**
   - Parallel streaming > faster network
   - CSS optimization > powerful hardware
   - Smart logic > complex platform

3. **Focus on your bottleneck**
   - Your bottleneck: Document intelligence
   - Not your bottleneck: Voice latency
   - Don't solve the wrong problem

4. **Simplicity wins**
   - Current setup: Simple, effective, ready
   - LiveKit setup: Complex, partial solution, 12+ months
   - Choose the right tool for the right job
